Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 | StealthAI is committed to the highest standards of data protection and transparency.

1. Information we collect

We collect business contact information (name, company, email, phone), billing details, and usage metadata (API volumes, performance metrics). We do NOT store or log your model inputs or outputs unless explicitly enabled for debugging.

2. How we use your information

To provide and maintain our secure AI services, billing, account management, security monitoring, and compliance reporting. We never use your data for model training without written consent.

3. Data retention

Customer account data retained while active. Usage logs anonymized after 30 days. You may request deletion at any time.

4. Third-party sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Limited sharing with infrastructure providers (AWS, Azure) under strict data processing agreements. All data remains encrypted.

5. Your rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability. Email privacy@stealthai.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. Security measures

AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, hardware security modules (HSMs), quarterly penetration tests, and zero-trust architecture.

7. Children's privacy

StealthAI is for business use only and not intended for individuals under 18.

8. International data transfers

We offer data residency options (US, EU, APAC). Standard Contractual Clauses govern cross-border transfers.

9. Policy changes & contact

Material changes will be notified via email. Data Protection Officer: dpo@stealthai.com. Mailing address: 111 Secure Way, Suite 500, Austin, TX 78701.
